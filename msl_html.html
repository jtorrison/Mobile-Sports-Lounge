Common Questions

Mobile Golf Simulator FAQ

Everything you need to know about renting a mobile golf simulator in Denver and Colorado.

How much does a mobile golf simulator cost to rent in Denver?

Mobile Sports Lounge starts at $325/hr — significantly less than competitors like WeGo ($1,500 for 2 hours) and Dryvebox ($500/hr). Our pricing is transparent and we offer hourly, half-day, and full-day packages. Add-ons like food, bar service, and an on-site host are available at additional cost. Contact us for a custom quote.

What technology do you use? Is it accurate?

We use Foresight and Garmin launch monitors — professional-grade technology trusted by PGA Tour players, top coaches, and serious golfers. This is a major upgrade from consumer systems like Skytrak that many competitors use. You get accurate ball speed, launch angle, spin rate, and carry distance data with every shot.

Do you travel outside of Denver?

Yes — we serve all of Colorado including Boulder, Colorado Springs, Fort Collins, Vail, Aspen, Breckenridge, and Cheyenne, Wyoming. Travel fees may apply for locations more than 30 miles from Denver. Contact us with your location and we'll give you a full quote.

What sports can you play besides golf?

Our E6 Interactive multi-sport system includes: golf, soccer, hockey, baseball, bowling, dodgeball, disc golf, foot golf, and wild west shooting gallery. Up to 8 players can compete simultaneously across all sports. It's the perfect icebreaker for any event — even non-golfers love it.

How much space do you need to set up?

We need a minimum ceiling height of 9-10 feet and roughly 15 feet of depth by 15 feet wide. Most hotel ballrooms, event spaces, offices, and large rooms work perfectly. Outdoor setups under a tent are also possible. Contact us with your venue and we'll confirm fit.

How far in advance do I need to book?

We recommend booking at least 2-4 weeks in advance for corporate events and weddings, especially for weekend dates. We can accommodate last-minute bookings when available — call or text Jacob directly at (720) 363-2960 to check availability.

Do you provide food and drinks?

We offer charcuterie and food packages as well as bar and drink package add-ons. These can be included in your custom package at an additional cost. For large events at licensed venues, we coordinate with the venue's bar service. Ask us about add-on options when you request a quote.

What makes you different from Dryvebox or WeGo Golf?

Three things: price, technology, and versatility. We're significantly more affordable than WeGo ($1,500 for 2 hours) and more feature-rich than Dryvebox. We use professional Foresight technology (not Skytrak), offer 9 sports beyond golf, provide an on-site host, and include food and drink add-ons. We're the full-service option.
